# SECTION 4 — 3-Minute Winning Demo Script

> **Goal:** maximize judge score on Innovation, Execution, Impact, Completeness, Presentation. **Demo two resumes, same role, opposite verdicts.** Rehearse to hit 2:55. Have `DEMO_MODE` pre-baked.

**Roles in the room:** 1 driver (talks + clicks). Pre-load Bilal's resume file + Aisha's resume file on the desktop.

---

### [0:00–0:20] Opening Hook
> "Every student asks three questions before they graduate: *Am I ready? What do I learn next? What do I build?* Today, those questions get answered by scattered Reddit threads and guesswork. **CareerPilot answers all three in 30 seconds — with evidence, not vibes.**"

*(On screen: landing page, cursor on "Analyze My Resume".)*

---

### [0:20–0:35] Problem Statement
> "75% of resumes are filtered before a human reads them, career counseling costs $100+ an hour, and generic AI just says 'improve your skills.' Students don't need a chatbot — they need a **grounded copilot** that knows exactly where they stand."

---

### [0:35–0:50] Product Walkthrough (one breath)
> "CareerPilot reads your resume, compares it against real role requirements with a **deterministic matching engine — no hallucinations** — and produces a Resume Health report, a Career Readiness score, an evidence-based skill gap, a 24-week roadmap, and an AI mentor you can actually talk to."

---

### [0:50–2:10] Live Demo Flow

**1. The struggling student (0:50–1:25)**
- Upload **Bilal (Weak Backend)** → select **Backend Developer**.
- *(Progress checklist animates.)* Dashboard loads.
- "Readiness: **40 out of 100.** Look — it's not vague. **Skill Gap**: he has JavaScript and Git (green), but he's missing Node.js, Express.js, REST APIs, MongoDB (red). This is the moment judges should note: **these are real, computed facts, not an LLM guessing.**"
- Scroll to **Roadmap**: "A 16-week plan — Node.js, then Express, then REST, then MongoDB — each week tied to a specific gap, with a project and a resource."

**2. The Mentor Wow Moment (1:25–2:00)**
- Open the **Mentor**. Type: **"Can I become a backend developer in 6 months?"**
- Read the grounded answer aloud: "It says — *yes, realistically* — and cites his actual skills, his missing four, and the exact roadmap weeks. **This is grounded in his resume, not the internet.**"
- Type: **"Should I learn Kubernetes?"** → "Watch — it **refuses** to recommend it because it's not in his plan. Most AI would happily hallucinate a yes. Ours won't."

**3. The success state (2:00–2:10)**
- Upload **Aisha (Strong Backend)** → "Same role, different person: **87 out of 100, 6 of 6 skills, all green, no roadmap needed** — 'apply now.' Same engine, honest opposite verdict."

---

### [2:10–2:35] Mentor Wow Moment (reinforce the differentiator)
> "That mentor is the heart of CareerPilot. It's grounded in a **deterministic engine** we built from scratch — alias-aware skill matching against six curated roles. The AI only *explains* facts it's handed; it can never invent a skill, a project, or a certification. **That's how we make AI career advice you can trust.**"

---

### [2:35–3:00] Closing Statement
> "CareerPilot turns 'I don't know where I stand' into 'I have a 16-week plan and the confidence to start.' It's free, private, no login — and it works for every student, not just the ones who can afford a coach. **We didn't build a chatbot. We built the career mentor every student deserves.** Thank you."
